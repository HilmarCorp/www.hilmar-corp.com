HilmarCorp joins KEDGE Entrepreneurship

HilmarCorp has joined the KEDGE Entrepreneurship program to accelerate its development and expand its network.

3 November 2025

Official announcement

HilmarCorp officially joins KEDGE Entrepreneurship, the incubator of KEDGE Business School. This milestone represents a key step in our institutional development: strengthening our regulatory alignment (PSAN, AMF), consolidating our innovation pipeline, and accelerating the growth of our AI-driven digital asset management model.

Beyond strategic guidance, the pre-incubation phase offers privileged access to a network of entrepreneurs, investors, and mentors across finance, data science, and technology.

It’s a pivotal step towards aligning our technological ambitions with robust governance and sustainable performance.
