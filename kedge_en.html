HilmarCorp joins KEDGE Entrepreneurship

HilmarCorp has joined the KEDGE Entrepreneurship program to accelerate its development and expand its network.

3 November 2025

Official announcement

HilmarCorp is proud to announce its entry into KEDGE Entrepreneurship. This support program from KEDGE Business School guides entrepreneurs at every stage of their development. KEDGE Entrepreneurship offers a rich ecosystem of mentors, resources and partners and was named “Best school for Entrepreneurship” by Forbes and Le Parisien in 2019.

Joining this incubator represents a strategic milestone for HilmarCorp. Through our collaboration with KEDGE we will benefit from a stimulating environment to continue developing our artificial intelligence solutions for digital asset management. We will gain access to workshops, technical resources and a network of entrepreneurs and experts who will enrich our work.

This integration reinforces our ambition to become a leading player in AI-driven digital asset management. Together with KEDGE Entrepreneurship, we will be able to accelerate our go-to-market efforts, share our innovations and actively contribute to the French and European entrepreneurial ecosystem.
