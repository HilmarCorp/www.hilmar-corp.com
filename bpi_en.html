Bpifrance Grant

HilmarCorp and Bpifrance join forces to accelerate financial innovation.

Bpifrance is France’s public investment bank that supports innovative companies at every stage of their development. We recently submitted a grant application to finance the training of a generative model specialized in simulating financial markets.

The financial support from Bpifrance will allow us to acquire powerful computing resources, build diversified data sets and hire new researchers to strengthen our team. These means are essential for developing high‑performing algorithms, testing our models on varied markets and accelerating our artificial intelligence roadmap.

Beyond financing, this partnership illustrates the confidence placed in our vision: democratizing access to advanced simulation and financial analysis tools. We sincerely thank Bpifrance and our partners for their support and guidance throughout this journey. We look forward to sharing our progress and contributing to the European technology ecosystem.
